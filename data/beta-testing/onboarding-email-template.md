## Known Limitations (M7)

This beta focuses on validating our core marking approach, so some features are limited:

- Only Pearson Edexcel GCSE Maths papers (other boards coming soon)
- Single-question marking (no full paper marking yet)
- One student at a time (batch marking coming in M8)
- No PDF/CSV export yet

These are planned improvements, not bugs!
