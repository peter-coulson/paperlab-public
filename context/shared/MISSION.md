# Mission

## Current Goal (6 months)

Become the primary past paper marking tool for GCSE students in the UK.

Starting with GCSE Maths (Pearson Edexcel), we provide instant, accurate marking with working analysis that identifies specific misconceptions.

**Success means:** Students know exactly what they got wrong, why they got it wrong, and how to improve.

## How We Achieve Our Mission

We make analytical insight available by providing accurate automated marking with working analysis that identifies specific misconceptions. This level of insight was previously only available from expensive tutors - we make it accessible to every student.

Our approach: serve students without compromise. Our target students are motivated, ambitious, and driven - they don't need authority or pressure. They need analytical intelligence that works for them, on their terms.

### Operating Principles

These principles define how we pursue our mission in practice:

#### 1. Student-Directed

Students control when, what, and how much they practice. We exist to serve their self-directed learning, not to prescribe a curriculum or enforce engagement. Everything is optional, nothing is mandatory.

**This means:**
- No forced tutorials or mandatory onboarding flows
- No guilt-trip notifications ("You haven't practiced in 3 days!")
- No streak counters or gamification that creates pressure
- No paywalls mid-workflow or bait-and-switch features
- Students can exit any flow at any time

**Litmus test:** "Does this respect student agency, or does it serve our engagement metrics?"

#### 2. On-Demand

We're available instantly when students need marking, and invisible when they don't. We're a tool in their arsenal, not their coach. They call upon us when needed - we never intrude uninvited.

**This means:**
- Instant marking results (no waiting, no queues)
- Always available (high uptime, reliable infrastructure)
- No intrusive reminders or unsolicited suggestions
- Students initiate all interactions (we respond, never push)
- Frictionless workflow (photo → mark → insight in seconds)

**Litmus test:** "Does this add friction or remove it?"

#### 3. Insight-Focused

We provide specific, actionable feedback that identifies exact failure points. Not "question 3 is wrong" but "you consistently make errors when converting mixed numbers to improper fractions - here's why and what to practice."

**This means:**
- Working analysis on every question (not just final answers)
- Precise identification of where understanding breaks down
- Pattern recognition across multiple questions
- Actionable next steps (what to practice, why it matters)
- Clear explanations (no vague mark schemes or jargon)

**Litmus test:** "Does this help students understand their mistakes, or just tell them they're wrong?"

## The Problem We're Solving

Past paper practice is how UK students prepare for exams. Current approaches fail students in three ways:

1. **Answer-only checking** - Students mark their own work against published mark schemes. They see final answers but miss the working, miss the insight into why they went wrong.

2. **Manual marking** - Teachers and tutors provide detailed feedback, but it's slow, expensive, and doesn't scale. Students can't practice freely without waiting for results.

3. **Generic feedback** - Existing tools give right/wrong marks but don't identify specific misconceptions. Students don't know what to fix or how to improve.

**Result:** Students practice ineffectively. They don't understand their mistakes. They repeat the same errors. They don't improve as fast as they could.

---

## Our Solution

We provide unlimited analytical insight through accurate, automated marking with working analysis. We make the kind of detailed feedback that was previously only available from expensive tutors accessible to every student.

**How it works:**
1. Students photograph handwritten work (mobile-first, frictionless)
2. LLMs analyze step-by-step working (not just final answers)
3. Students receive instant marking + precise insight into mistakes
4. Pattern detection reveals recurring misconceptions across questions

**Why now:** This wasn't possible before 2023. LLMs with large context windows can now understand handwritten working and identify subtle errors that traditional software couldn't detect. We can provide unlimited analytical resources at a fraction of traditional tutoring costs.

**Our advantage:** Working analysis at scale. Understanding the "why" behind mistakes, not just marking right/wrong. Every student gets world-class analytical support.

## Who We Serve

### Our Vision: Every Student

We exist to help **every student** achieve their highest possible grade, regardless of starting ability. A student aiming for grade 5 deserves the same quality of insight as one aiming for grade 9. Understanding mistakes is universal - it helps everyone improve.

### Initial Focus: High-Achieving Students (Ages 15-18)

**This is a go-to-market strategy, not a limitation.**

We're starting with ambitious students preparing for GCSE Maths (Pearson Edexcel) as our beachhead market. These students:
- Already do past papers regularly (we're not convincing them to start)
- Care deeply about improving (intrinsically motivated)
- Pursue high grades (8-9, not just passing)
- Respond to precision and specificity (want to know exactly what went wrong)
- Value their time (need instant feedback, not delays)

**Why start with them:**
- They're already motivated (validates product-market fit quickly)
- They value quality feedback (willing to pay for insight)
- They practice frequently (high engagement, rapid feedback loop)
- They refer others (word-of-mouth growth in schools)
- They're the clearest early adopters (de-risks launch)

Once we've proven the approach works with this group, we'll expand to serve students at all ability levels. The technology works regardless of target grade - analytical insight helps everyone improve.

### Economic Buyers: Parents

Parents pay for the subscription. They care about:
- **Effectiveness** - Will this actually improve grades?
- **Trustworthiness** - Is the marking accurate and reliable?
- **Value** - Is this worth the investment compared to tutoring?
- **Safety** - Is my child's data secure?

### Legitimizers: Exam Boards

Alignment with official exam boards (starting with Pearson Edexcel) builds trust with both students and parents. We use official mark schemes, grade boundaries, and question types.

### Amplifiers: Schools and Teachers

Schools can become customers (whole-cohort licenses) and advocates (recommendations to students). Teachers care about:
- **Accuracy** - Does this match official marking standards?
- **Scalability** - Can this reduce their marking workload?
- **Insight** - Does this help identify student weaknesses at cohort level?
