## Introduction

PaperLab ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

PaperLab is an AI-powered exam marking tool designed to help GCSE students practice and learn from their exam responses. The App is intended for users aged 13 and above.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you create an account and use PaperLab, we collect:

- **Email Address:** Used for account creation, authentication, and communication
- **Account Credentials:** Encrypted password for secure access

### Content You Provide

- **Exam Paper Images:** Photos of past exam papers and student work that you upload for marking
- **Marking Sessions:** Records of papers you've marked, including questions attempted and results

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, and app version
- **Usage Data:** Features accessed, session duration, and interaction patterns
- **Camera and Photo Library Access:** Required to capture and upload exam paper images

### Usage Analytics

To improve the App, we may collect:

- Technical performance metrics
- Feature usage patterns
- Error logs and crash reports

This data helps us identify issues and improve the user experience.

### Data Linking

The following data is **linked to your identity** (connected to your account):
- Email address and account credentials
- Uploaded exam paper images
- Marking sessions and results
- Usage data and interaction patterns

The following data is **not linked to your identity**:
- Aggregated performance metrics
- Anonymous crash reports (when personally identifiable information is removed)

## Third-Party AI Processing

**IMPORTANT: Your exam paper images and marking data will be sent to third-party AI services for processing.**

To provide automated marking functionality, PaperLab uses artificial intelligence services operated by **OpenAI**, **Anthropic**, and **Google (Gemini)**. When you upload an exam paper image for marking:

1. **Your images are sent to these third-party AI providers** for analysis and marking
2. **Marking data and results are processed** by these AI systems
3. **You must explicitly consent** to this data sharing before uploading any images

### What This Means

- Your exam paper images leave our servers and are transmitted to OpenAI, Anthropic, and/or Google
- These companies process your images using their AI models to generate marking feedback
- This processing is **essential** for the app to function - without it, automated marking cannot occur

### Your Consent

By uploading images to PaperLab, you explicitly consent to:
- Sharing your exam paper images with OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google
- Processing of this data by third-party AI systems
- Temporary storage of your data on third-party servers during processing

You can withdraw consent at any time by discontinuing use of the app and requesting data deletion.

### Third-Party AI Data Retention

- **OpenAI:** Images sent to OpenAI's API are not used for model training and are retained for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring, then automatically deleted
- **Anthropic:** Images sent to Anthropic's API are not used for model training and are retained for 7-30 days (depending on configuration) for trust and safety purposes, then automatically deleted
- **Google (Gemini):** Images sent to Google's Gemini API are not used for model training when using the paid API and are retained temporarily for processing, then automatically deleted

**Important:** None of these providers use your API data for model training.

For more information, see:
- [OpenAI Enterprise Privacy](https://openai.com/enterprise-privacy/)
- [Anthropic API Data Retention](https://privacy.claude.com/en/articles/10023548-how-long-do-you-store-my-data)
- [Google Gemini API Data Privacy](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms)

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage

- **Uploaded Images:** Stored securely in Cloudflare R2 (cloud storage) with encryption in transit and at rest
- **Account Data:** Stored in Supabase (our authentication provider) with industry-standard encryption
- **Geographic Location:** Data is stored in the United States

### Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encrypted data storage
- Secure authentication mechanisms
- Regular security assessments

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

### PaperLab's Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active
- **Uploaded Images:** Retained indefinitely until you delete them or request account deletion. Images are stored securely in Cloudflare R2 and remain accessible for your review and analysis
- **Marking Results:** Retained while your account is active to enable review and analysis

### How to Delete Your Data

You may delete your data at any time:

- **Delete Account:** Use the "Delete Account" button in the app settings to permanently delete your account and all associated data (images, marking results, account information). This is immediate and cannot be undone.
- **Contact Support:** Alternatively, you can contact us at support@mypaperlab.com to request manual deletion of your account and data.

When your account is deleted:
1. Your data is removed from our systems immediately
2. Backups may retain data for up to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes, after which they are permanently purged

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers:** Third-party services that help us operate the App (e.g., Supabase for authentication, Cloudflare for storage and web hosting, Railway for backend hosting)

- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law, court order, or legal process
- **Safety and Security:** To protect the rights, property, or safety of PaperLab, our users, or others
- **Business Transfers:** In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified)

## Tracking

**PaperLab does not track you.** We do not link data collected from our app with third-party data for targeted advertising or marketing purposes. We do not sell your data to data brokers or use it for cross-app tracking.

While we use third-party services (Cloudflare, Railway, OpenAI, Anthropic) to operate the app, we do not share your data with advertising networks or analytics services that track you across other apps or websites.

## Camera and Photo Library Permissions

PaperLab requires access to your device's camera and photo library to enable the core functionality of the app.

### Camera Access

**Purpose:** "PaperLab needs camera access to capture images of exam papers for automated marking analysis."

We request camera permission so you can:
- Take photos of exam papers directly within the app
- Capture clear images optimized for marking analysis

### Photo Library Access

**Purpose:** "PaperLab needs photo library access to select previously captured exam papers for marking."

We request photo library permission so you can:
- Select previously taken photos of exam papers from your device
- Upload saved images for marking analysis

### Your Control

- We **only** access your camera or photo library when you explicitly choose to take a photo or select an image
- We **do not** access your camera or photo library in the background
- We **do not** access any photos other than those you specifically select for upload
- You can revoke camera and photo library permissions at any time in your device Settings (though this will prevent you from uploading exam papers)
